PlanJapan is a website developed by Sharon Sulema, Gal Nissanzon, Rotem Bokobza, and Yali Tuchler.
Your trusted guide to exploring the beauty and culture of Japan.


About Our Website

We created this website as a project for the 'Website Development' course during our first year of studies in 2024. This website was designed and developed according to UI design principles, using HTML5 and CSS3, with careful attention to web accessibility standards. Our goal was to assist individuals in planning their trips to Japan. Throughout the development process, we faced challenges in collaboration and synchronizing independently created HTML pages. Nevertheless, we are proud to present the final outcome and hope you enjoy exploring it.


Who Is It For?

This website is designed for affluent adults aged 45-60 who have a cultivated appreciation for Japan's unique cultural and luxury experiences. With older children, they now have the time, freedom, and resources to invest in thoughtfully curated journeys.
